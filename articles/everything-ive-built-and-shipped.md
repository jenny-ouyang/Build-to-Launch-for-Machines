# Everything I've Built and Shipped: 16 AI Apps, MCPs, and Services

**Track:** Products Built and Shipped
**Published:** 2026-04-21

## Summary

This is the complete product catalog for Build to Launch — every AI app, MCP, and service Jenny has shipped since January 2025, organized into six categories. The article explicitly includes retired products and internal tools because, as Jenny puts it, the honest picture matters more than a curated highlight reel.

The largest category is Substack growth tools: five products that each pair a hosted web app with a Claude or Cursor MCP. Quick Viral Notes generates note variants from any article URL and schedules them into a queue. Substack Explorer pulls newsletter analytics and post histories for niche research. Postingly publishes to Bluesky, LinkedIn, and Threads simultaneously. resources.buildtolaunch.ai is the prompt and skill library bundled with every paid subscription. And the Substack Article MCP (free, npm-installable) retrieves paywalled articles you're subscribed to for use inside Claude.

For AI builders, the catalog includes tools.buildtolaunch.ai — a 10-app interactive suite covering research prompts, project planning, website auditing, SEO, guardrail generation, and pricing — plus the free Vibe Coding Builders community directory at vibecoding.builders and the open-source multi-account Gmail MCP. Personal and consumer apps include jennyouyang.ai (RAG-powered semantic search across all articles), a Color Vision Enhancer for accessibility, and Dealist (MyDealHunter) for automated price tracking.

Two products are retired: Image Finder (natural-language search for local images), which taught the lesson that 90% of shipping a product isn't writing code, and Life Pivot (an AI life-simulator), which worked but didn't compound. Two tools are listed as internal and not for sale: a private language-learning app for Jenny's kids, and the ai-agent-memory MCP that maintains persistent context across every Claude session.
